Bacon and Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • Pasta (1 pound)
  • Chicken (2 breast)
  • Bacon (1 Pound)
  • Bertolli Alfredo Sauce (1 jar)
  • Spinach (optional)

Simple Instructions

Cook all ingredients separately, chop up chicken and bacon, and then mix all ingredients together.
