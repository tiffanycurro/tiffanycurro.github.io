Dill Grilled Salmon

Ingredients:

  • Fresh Salmon (1 pound)
  • Minced Garlic (4 cloves)
  • Fresh Dill (1 bushel)
  • Olive Oil (2-3 tbs.)

Simple Instructions

Preheat grill to 350 degrees, cover bottom of pan with olive oil, place salmon fillets on pan, then top them each with garlic and dill. Let cook for 15 minutes and then serve. This dish goes well with rice and fresh veggies.
